Burger

Directions

  1. Gather your ingredients: ground beef, salt, pepper, buns, lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and condiments.
  2. Preheat your grill or skillet over medium heat.
  3. Form the ground beef into patties, about 1/2 inch thick.
  4. Season both sides of the patties with salt and pepper.
  5. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side for medium doneness.
  6. While the patties are cooking, prepare your buns and toppings.
  7. Once the patties are cooked to your liking, remove them from the heat and let them rest for a few minutes.
  8. Assemble your burgers with the patties, lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and your favorite condiments.
  9. Enjoy your homemade burger!
