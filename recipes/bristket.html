Brisket

Directions

  1. Gather your ingredients: a beef brisket, salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, and barbecue sauce.
  2. Preheat your oven to 300 degrees Fahrenheit.
  3. Season the brisket on all sides with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder.
  4. Place the brisket in a roasting pan and cover it with foil.
  5. Cook the brisket in the oven for about 3 hours, or until it's tender.
  6. Remove the foil, brush the brisket with barbecue sauce, and return it to the oven for another 30 minutes.
  7. Once the brisket is cooked, remove it from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing.
  8. Enjoy your homemade brisket!
