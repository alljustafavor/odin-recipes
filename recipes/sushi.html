Sushi

Directions

  1. Gather your ingredients: sushi rice, nori (seaweed sheets), fresh fish, vegetables, soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.
  2. Prepare the sushi rice according to the package instructions.
  3. Cut the fresh fish and vegetables into thin slices.
  4. Place a sheet of nori on a bamboo sushi mat, then spread a thin layer of sushi rice on top.
  5. Arrange the fish and vegetables on top of the rice.
  6. Roll the sushi tightly using the bamboo mat.
  7. Use a sharp knife to slice the sushi roll into bite-sized pieces.
  8. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.
  9. Enjoy your homemade sushi!
