Chicken Parm

Directions

  1. Gather your ingredients: chicken breasts, salt, pepper, flour, eggs, breadcrumbs, parmesan cheese, marinara sauce, and mozzarella cheese.
  2. Preheat your oven to 375 degrees Fahrenheit.
  3. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then dredge them in flour, dip them in beaten eggs, and coat them in a mixture of breadcrumbs and parmesan cheese.
  4. Cook the chicken in a skillet over medium heat until golden brown on both sides.
  5. Place the chicken in a baking dish, top with marinara sauce and mozzarella cheese, and bake until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  6. While the chicken is baking, prepare your side dishes.
  7. Once the chicken is cooked, remove it from the oven and let it rest for a few minutes.
  8. Enjoy your homemade chicken parm!
